WELCOME TO BHARAT AGRO EXPORT

BHARAT AGRO EXPORT was established in the year 2022 in Gujarat, India. We are an Manufacturer, Exporter & Trader of plant growth promoters, liquid solutions, and a lot more. Our range is acknowledged for attributes like safe usage, effectiveness, precise pH value and accurate chemical composition. These biotechnology products can also be used as solvents in paints, fuels and lube additives.

Our comprehensive gamut of products is developed using plant-based alkaloids, which are combined with microbial enzymes to make these highly suitable for protection against various pests and plant diseases. These products are formulated as per the set quality norms using the most advanced methodologies and techniques.

Furthermore, it is also ensured that these do not leave any residue after application. Also, these are processed in such a manner to make these completely environment-friendly, which makes these most preferred by our clients.
