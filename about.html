WELCOME TO BHARAT AGRO EXPORT

Established in 2022 in Gujarat, India, Bharat Agro Export is a trusted Manufacturer, Exporter & Trader specializing in plant growth promoters, liquid solutions, and more. Our product range is acclaimed for its safety, effectiveness, precise pH values, and accurate chemical compositions. These biotechnology products also find applications as solvents in industries such as paints, fuels, and lube additives.

Our comprehensive selection of products is meticulously crafted using plant-based alkaloids, combined with microbial enzymes to offer effective protection against various pests and plant diseases. Employing state-of-the-art methodologies and techniques, we ensure that our products meet the highest quality standards.

Furthermore, we take pride in our commitment to producing products that leave no residue after application and are environmentally friendly. This dedication makes our offerings the preferred choice among our valued clients.
